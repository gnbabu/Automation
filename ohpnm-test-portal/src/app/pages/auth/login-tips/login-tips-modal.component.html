Login Assistance & Tips

Review these quick tips to ensure a secure and successful login to the PNM Automation Portal.

  • Use SSO: For maximum security and convenience, always prefer the "Sign In With Maximus SSO" option.
  • Clear Credentials: If you are using a shared or public device, ensure you uncheck the device persistence box below the password field.
  • Check Caps Lock: Passwords are case-sensitive. Ensure your Caps Lock key is not accidentally enabled.
  • Account Recovery: If you encounter issues, use the "Forgot user name?" or "Forgot password?" links to quickly regain access to your account.
