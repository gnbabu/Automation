Registration Guidelines

Follow these guidelines to successfully create your account for the PNM Automation Portal.

  • Username: Choose a unique identifier. This is what you will use to Sign In if you don't use SSO.
  • Email Address: Must be a valid email address. This is essential for verification, account recovery, and official communications.
  • Password Strength: Ensure your password meets the security requirements (usually length, mixed cases, numbers, and symbols).
  • Shared Devices: If you are registering from a shared or public computer, avoid saving credentials and log out after use.
